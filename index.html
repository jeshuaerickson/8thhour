About the Band

8th Hour plays music by Queen, Wilco, Seals & Crofts, The Marshall Tucker Band, Van Morrison, Grateful Dead, Tom Petty, Traveling Wilburys, Buffalo Springfield, REM, Bruce Springsteen, Tyler Childers, Cyndi Lauper, Bob Dylan, Jackson Browne, Avett Brothers, Tears for Fears, Paul Simon, Indigo Girls, Jason Isbell, Bob Marley, Whiskeytown, Eagles, The Band, Marvin Gaye, The Allman Brothers Band, Nick Lowe, Mandolin Orange, Simon & Garfunkel, The Decemberists, The Beatles, Sheryl Crow, John Prine, U2, Neil Young, The Replacements, Foo Fighters, Dire Straits, Boston, ABBA, The Cure, Grant-Lee Buffalo, Bruce Cockburn, The Lumineers, Prince, Chappell Roan ... and many, many more.

Band Statement

"8th Hour is what happens when three teachers, a pastor, and an IT guy walk into a jam session and leave later than they should. Based in southern Minnesota, we’re a group of friends who play familiar tunes spanning folk, light rock, and Americana — from Bob Dylan and Simon & Garfunkel to Prince, Chappell Roan, and the Indigo Girls. With a lineup of violin, bass, drum, and two acoustic guitars, we keep things laid-back, full of harmony, and rooted in the joy of making music together. We don’t take ourselves too seriously — we just love a good song and a better vibe. Come hang out and sing along."
