About the Band

8th Hour blends raw indie rock energy with melodic hooks and heartfelt lyrics. Formed in 2022, the band quickly gained a loyal following with their electrifying live shows and debut EP, Midnight Sun. With influences ranging from The Strokes to Florence + the Machine, 8th Hour is carving out a unique sound all their own.
